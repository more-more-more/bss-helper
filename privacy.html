Privacy Policy – BSS-Helper Discord Bot

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

BSS-Helper collects minimal data needed for functionality: Discord user IDs, server IDs, and interaction data. Messages are not stored permanently.

2. How Information Is Used

Data is used solely to respond to commands, improve performance, and maintain security.

3. Data Storage & Retention

Data is temporarily stored in memory and discarded when no longer needed.

4. Data Sharing

No personal data is sold, shared, or rented. Data may be processed by third-party services only as required.

5. Third-Party Services

The Bot interacts with Discord API, AI APIs, and public Fandom/Wiki websites. Each service has its own privacy policy.

6. User Rights

Users may remove the Bot at any time and can request clarification regarding data usage.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Bot is not intended for users below Discord’s minimum age requirement.

8. Changes to This Policy

Continued use constitutes acceptance of updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions? Contact the developer via Discord.
