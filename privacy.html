1. Information We Collect

BSS-Helper collects minimal data necessary to provide its services. This includes:

  • Discord User IDs: For identifying users when interacting with the bot.
  • Server IDs: To distinguish between different Discord servers and apply server-specific settings.
  • Temporary Interaction Data: Short-term data used to process commands and generate responses.

Important: No personal messages, passwords, or sensitive private information are collected or stored permanently.

2. How We Use Information

The data we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Responding to commands issued by users
  • Improving bot performance and stability
  • Maintaining security and preventing misuse
  • Debugging errors or technical issues when they arise

3. Data Storage & Retention

All collected data is stored temporarily in memory and is automatically deleted after it is no longer needed to fulfill the command or process. No long-term logs of user messages are maintained.

4. Data Sharing

BSS-Helper does not sell, rent, or trade any personal information. Data may be shared with trusted third-party services only to the extent necessary to operate the bot, such as:

  • Discord API (for message handling and command processing)
  • AI APIs (for generating summaries or responses)
  • Public Fandom/Wiki pages (to provide game information)

All third-party services have their own privacy policies, and use of the bot implies acceptance of those policies.

5. Cookies & Tracking

BSS-Helper itself does not use cookies or tracking scripts. However, if you access links to third-party websites, those sites may use cookies, trackers, or analytics independently of the bot.

6. Children’s Privacy

The bot is intended for users who meet Discord’s minimum age requirement (usually 13+). We do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian believes their child has provided data to the bot, they may contact us to request deletion.

7. User Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • To remove the bot from your server at any time
  • To request clarification on what data is collected and how it is used
  • To request deletion of any temporary interaction data associated with you

8. Security Measures

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no online service can be completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted through Discord or third-party APIs.
